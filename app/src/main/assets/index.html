Welcome to Arted!

Arted is a simple and fun drawing app that lets you unleash your creativity. Here's how to get started:

Drawing Basics

  1. Selecting a Color: Tap on one of the color circles in the toolbar at the top of the screen to choose your drawing color. A toast will appear to confirm your selection.

  2. Adjusting Brush Size: Use the text field next to the color picker to set your brush size in pixels. Type in the desired size and press enter. The brush size will be updated immediately.

  3. Drawing: Simply drag your finger across the white canvas to start drawing. The app will create a line following your finger's movement.

  4. Erasing: To erase, tap the "Eraser" button in the expanded menu. The brush will now erase what you draw. To draw again, select a color in the color picker.

  5. Clearing the Canvas: If you want to start over, tap the "Reset" button in the expanded menu. This will clear the entire canvas.

Saving Your Drawings

  1. Saving: Once you're happy with your drawing, tap the "Save" button in the expanded menu. Your drawing will be saved to your device's gallery.

  2. Finding Your Drawings: You can find your saved drawings in your device's gallery app, usually in a folder called "Pictures/Arted".

Other Features

  • Expandable Menu: Tap the settings icon (gear) in the toolbar to expand the menu. This menu contains the "Eraser," "Reset," "Save," and "Subpage" buttons.

  • Subpage: Tap the "Subpage" button in the expanded menu to access this manual.

Tips and Tricks

  • Experiment with different brush sizes to create various effects.

  • Use the eraser to correct mistakes or create unique patterns.
